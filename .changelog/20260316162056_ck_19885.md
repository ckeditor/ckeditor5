---
type: Major breaking change
scope:
  - ckeditor5-editor-multi-root
see:
  - https://github.com/ckeditor/ckeditor5/issues/19885
---

The `config.rootsAttributes` and `config.lazyRoots` multi-root editor config options have been removed.
Use the `config.roots` option instead to define the editor roots and their attributes.

Migrate `config.rootsAttributes` to `config.roots.<rootName>.modelAttributes` to define the attributes of a root.
Migrate `config.lazyRoots` to `config.roots.<rootName>.lazyLoad` to define whether a root should be lazy loaded.

Note: The `config.roots.<rootName>.lazyLoad` property is deprecated and will be removed in a future version.
