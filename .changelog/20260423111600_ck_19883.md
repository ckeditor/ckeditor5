Improved how table cell alignment is loaded into the editor. Previously, applying alignment to a cell could incorrectly force text alignment on all nested elements inside it. Now, alignment is smartly applied to the direct contents of the cell. Whether an element uses block alignment or text alignment, it will be positioned appropriately without breaking the layout of deeper nested content.
