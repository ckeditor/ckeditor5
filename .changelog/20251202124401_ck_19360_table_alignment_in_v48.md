After adding the new block-level alignment options (`blockLeft` and `blockRight`), the editor now recognizes and upcasts the corresponding inline block-alignment styles (`margin-left:0;margin-right:auto;` and `margin-left:auto;margin-right:0;`) into these alignment types.
