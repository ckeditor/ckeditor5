Images are now supported in inline roots. A block image that cannot be placed at a given location (for example, when pasting, dropping, or loading data into an inline root) now degrades to an inline image instead of being dropped. The image type change, caption, and style controls adapt accordingly and become unavailable when the conversion they require is not allowed at the current position.
