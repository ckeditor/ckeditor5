Ingredients

Ingredients required for making a cup of coffee:

  • 15 g ground coffee
  • water
  • milk (optional)

Instructions

How to prepare a cup of coffee:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Put 15 g of ground coffee in the cup.
  3. Boil 200 ml of water.
  4. Pour the water into the cup.
  5. Optional: Add milk.
